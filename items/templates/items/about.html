About

BringMe is a website where users can be both a rider or a recipient. How can you use this website? For example, you're a teenager who lives in Gasan, Marinduque and you have something important to buy at Mercury Drug Store which is in Boac. You won't be able to buy at Mercury Drug Store because you're not yet 21 -- the police might catch you. So you post your item on this website.

Other users who live in Boac will see them, and they can decide whether to deliver to you your item or not. Other users can earn from delivering items because they will ask for their pay. You pay the other users according to how much they bought your item and how much they charge you for their service.

You will be notified through your email. If you're a rider, an email will be sent to you containing the link to new posts near you. If you're a recipient and a rider closed your post (secured your post), you will be emailed that your post has been closed and your item is probably on its way.

This website was built using Python and Django. It's repository is here.
